About Me

I work at the intersection of Computational Linguistics, Psycholinguistics and Pragmatics. Focused on Empathy, I have been conducting computational modeling and NLG studies on conversation and eye-movement. For example, I use LLMs to generate human-like empathy and reading behavior.

Before my PolyU days, I was major in Arabic and Middle East Studies. My experience in doing media data analysis and constructing speech corpus in Arabic sparked my curiosity about NLP and language models. I am open for any possibility to conduct NLP studies in Arabic languages.
